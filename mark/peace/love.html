Kevin Hart is an American comedian, actor, and producer known for his energetic humor and charismatic performances. Born on July 6, 1979, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, Hart has become one of the most successful comedians in the world.

He began his career in comedy clubs and quickly gained popularity with his stand-up specials, such as "I'm a Grown Little Man" and "Seriously Funny." Hart's comedic style often revolves around his personal life, relationships, and observations about everyday situations.
